# Contact

If you're considering therapy for a young person in your life, the best first step is to get in touch. There's no commitment — just a conversation.

## How to reach me

The easiest way to make first contact is by **email** at **[hello@lotushousetherapy.com](mailto:hello@lotushousetherapy.com)**. I aim to reply **within two working days**.

After that first exchange, we'll arrange a free 20-minute phone or Zoom consultation to talk things through.

## What to include in your enquiry

To help me respond usefully, please include:

- **Your child's age**
- **A short sentence** about what you're seeking help with
- **Daytime contact details** (email and phone)
- **A preferred time** to talk, if you have one

You don't need to write a long history — a few sentences is plenty. We'll cover the rest in the consultation.

## What happens next

1. I'll reply to your enquiry within two working days.
2. We'll arrange a **free 20-minute consultation** by phone or Zoom — a chance to talk through what's bringing you in touch and ask any practical questions.
3. If it feels like a good fit, we'll book a **first assessment session** with the young person.
4. After **four sessions**, we'll pause together to consider whether to continue.

I look forward to hearing from you.
